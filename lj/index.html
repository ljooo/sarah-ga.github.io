getting there

Take the Air

The nearest airports are Albany (ALB) and Newburgh (SWF) and from either, the site is a one-hour drive.

Take the Roads

The nearest city is, of course, NYC. It’s a two and a half hour ride that goes from concrete to green.

Take the Rails

You can take Amtrak to Rhinecliff, NY and snag a taxi, but you’ll probably need a car to get around.
