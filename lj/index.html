Travel

take the air

The nearest airport is Albany (ALB) and from there, the site is a 45 minute drive.

take the roads

The nearest city is, of course, NYC. It’s a two and a half hour ride that goes from concrete to green.

take the rails

You can take Amtrak to Rhinecliff, NY (RHI) and snag a taxi to your hotel, but you’ll probably need a car (or friends with cars!) to get around.
