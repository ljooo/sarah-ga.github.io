Sublime Text is really great you guys


What can Sublime Text do for us?

  1. It turns our tags pink.
  2. It numbers our lines of code.
  3. It suggests tags while we type.
  4. It lets us tab our nested elements.
  5. It draws lines between open and close tags.
  6. It lets us preview our code in a browser.

What can't Sublime Text do for us?

  1. Spell check!
  2. Style our fonts*

*who cares, we'll learn this in CSS and it'll be awesome.
