Friday, October 24, 2014

Fall is in full swing at Little Sprouts! We've been busy collecting leaves, learning about the lifecycle of trees, and wearing our red and orange crayons down to nubs.

This week, we learned all about fire safety from the brave men and women of the South Counties Volunteer Fire Department. Our sprouts practiced stopping, dropping, and rolling and learned that as soon as you smell smoke or see fire, you get out and stay out!

We also climbed aboard a fire engine, decorated our own fire hats, and talked about the importance of making a family emergency plan. This afternoon, you'll find a template in your child's folder that you can fill out together and post in your home. Invite your child to help figure out the quickest, safest routes you might use to escape in case of a fire, and let them lead a few test runs.
